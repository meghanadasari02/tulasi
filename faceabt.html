FACE MASK DETECTION

The proposed face mask detection platform utilizes artificial network to identify the person with and without mask. Mask detection models ensuring the efficient use of the masks at workplaces, educational institutes, hospitals, malls, airports.As surveillance performer it need to detect the faces in a classroom and if the person who is not wearing a mask will get a pop-up message reminding him/her to wear the mask.
